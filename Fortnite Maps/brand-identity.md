## Logo Style Direction

- **Primary Mark:** Geometric lettermark ‚Äî stylized "LG" monogram inside a hexagonal portal shape
- **Style:** Clean lines, slight glitch/distortion effect on one edge
- **Icon Version:** The portal shape alone (works as profile pic at small sizes)
- **Animation:** For intros/TikTok watermark ‚Äî the portal "opens" with a glitch effect, letters materialize
- **Font Pairing:** Bold geometric sans-serif (like Bebas Neue or Rajdhani) for headers, clean sans (Inter) for body

## Creator Persona

**Voice & Tone:**
- Confident but not arrogant ‚Äî "we figured out something wild" energy
- Slightly mysterious/dark (matches horror brand) but fun and accessible
- Behind-the-scenes honesty ‚Äî show the grind, the failures, the breakthroughs
- Never begging for plays/follows ‚Äî let the work speak

**Content Personality Archetype:** *The Mad Scientist Builder*
- "What if I combined X with Y?" curiosity-driven content
- Shows the creative process, not just the result
- Treats each map like an experiment
- Celebrates when things go hilariously wrong

**Audience:** 13-24, Fortnite Creative enthusiasts, aspiring map makers, horror game fans, tycoon addicts

**Brand Promise:** "Every map is an experience you've never had before."
